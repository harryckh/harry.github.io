Biography

I am a Postdoc Researcher in the Programming, Logic and Intelligent Systems (PLIS) research group in the Department of People and Technology, Roskilde University (RUC), Denmark. Currently, I am working on the research project Data Management for Indoor LBS (PI: Prof. Hua Lu). I graduated in Aug 2019 with a PhD degree from Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The University of Science and Technology (HKUST), Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Raymond Chi-Wing Wong. I obtained my BEng and MPhil degrees in Computer Science and Engineering in HKUST in 2013 and 2015, respectively.

In Fall 2019, I was a Visiting Lecturer in Hong Kong Community College (HKCC), The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU). From April 2019 to July 2019, I was a visiting student of Prof. Cheng Long, at School of Computer Science and Engineering (SCSE), Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. Besides, I was the hall tutor in Chan Sui Kau and Chan Lam Moon Chun Hall (UG Hall VII), HKUST from Feb 2016 to Apr 2019.

Research Interests

My research interests include spatial data management, query processing, knowledge discovery, data mining and big data. I worked on the problem of query processing on spatio-textual data. I also worked on problem of mining spatial co-located patterns. Currently I am working in the area of indoor location-based services (LBS).
